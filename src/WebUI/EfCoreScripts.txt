Add migration:
dotnet ef migrations add "InitialCreate" --project src\Infrastructure --startup-project src\Web --output-dir Data\Migrations

Remove migration:
dotnet ef migrations remove "InitialCreate" --project src\Infrastructure --startup-project src\Web --output-dir Data\Migrations

Update database:
dotnet ef database update --project src\Infrastructure --startup-project src\Web --output-dir Data\Migrations

Drop database:
dotnet ef database drop --project src\Infrastructure --startup-project src\Web --output-dir Data\Migrations
